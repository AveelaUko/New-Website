About Us

Aveela Groups Inc. was conceived in 2021 as a private Company and commenced business that same year. The Company started as a small but disciplined entity working mainly as a web design service,engineer services and HSE Consultants; then as a small contracts handler and is gradually growing until today. From the beginning, Aveela Groups started out as a civil engineering/HSE company; today, it is still growing to become a major player in the building profession, and to be responsible for some unmistakable landmarks.
